Many pet owners are not able to easily spot when their beloved dog has, or is developing, osteoarthritis (OA).

They can often misinterpret the signs – or simply fail to observe them – meaning pets are not given the care they need at the relevant stage in this progressive condition.

This is why we’ve launched a campaign targeted at the pet owner called ‘Join the Movement’ to build owner awareness of OA, help them identify the possible symptoms and drive them to you, their vet, for a diagnosis and management plan.
